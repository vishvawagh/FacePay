About Us

We are a dedicated team of innovators committed to making financial technology accessible to all, particularly to underserved and underprivileged communities. Our mission is to develop solutions that make digital transactions secure, easy, and inclusive, especially for those who face challenges in accessing traditional banking services.

Our Vision:

We envision a future where digital payments are within everyone's reach, regardless of their location, economic status, or technical knowledge. By leveraging advanced technologies like facial recognition, voice commands, and regional language support, we aim to simplify transactions and make digital payments a part of everyday life for everyone.

Our Approach:

Understanding the unique needs of underprivileged communities drives our approach. We have designed a web application that allows shopkeepers to authenticate themselves using normal face recognition and facilitates customer transactions through smile face authentication. For added convenience, customers can check their balance using normal face scanning directly from the homepage.

To ensure accessibility, our platform includes a toggle button on every page that allows users to switch the interface language, currently offering Marathi. This ensures that users can navigate the system with ease, in a language they understand.

Key Features and Workflow:

Shopkeeper Registration and Login:

  • Registration Process: Shopkeepers begin by accessing the sign-up page, where they provide their name, mobile number, and upload a photo. After successful registration, the shopkeeper can log in to the system using facial recognition. If the camera fails to capture a clear image, a PIN-based authentication is provided as a backup.
  • Voice Command Functionality: Recognizing that many users in rural areas may struggle with typing or navigating digital interfaces, our system offers voice command interactions. Shopkeepers can provide required information through voice input, making the process more accessible.

Home Page and Customer Interaction:

  • Shopkeeper Dashboard: After logging in, the shopkeeper is directed to their home page, which displays their profile details, including balance and transaction history. The dashboard also includes an option for Customer Sign-Up, where customers can register by providing their name, mobile number, and photo.
  • Customer Transaction Process: For transactions, the customerâ€™s number is entered, followed by a smiling face scan to authorize the payment. The system ensures that transactions are only successful after this dual-layer authentication.
  • Balance Check: Customers can also check their balance on the homepage using normal face authentication, making it easy for them to manage their funds and also check the history.

User Interface and Accessibility:

  • Regional Language Support: A toggle button is provided on every page to switch the interface language to Marathi, catering to users who are more comfortable with regional languages.
  • User-Friendly Design: The application is designed with simple icons and an intuitive layout, ensuring that it is easy to navigate for users with varying levels of digital literacy.

Why We Do/What We Do:

We recognize the challenges many people face when making small transactions, especially those under 100 rupees. Our solution is tailored to address this need, providing a simple and secure way for users to conduct low-value transactions without the complexity often associated with digital payments. We believe that even the smallest transactions should be made with ease and security.
